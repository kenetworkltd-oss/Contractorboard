Frequently Asked Questions

For Homeowners
Register as a Homeowner, log in, go to your Dashboard and click "Post a Job". Fill in the title, description, niche, location and budget.
Once your job is posted, contractors can send you inquiries. You will receive an email notification and can view all inquiries in your Dashboard.
Yes — posting jobs on Jobboard is completely free for homeowners.
Go to the contractor's profile page and scroll down to the reviews section. You can leave a star rating and a comment.
For Contractors
Click Register on the homepage and select "Register as Contractor". Fill in your business name, niche, and service area.
Browse jobs at /jobs/, click on any open job, and click "Send Inquiry". Write your message describing your experience, availability and estimated cost.
Verification is managed by our admin team. Contact us and we will review your profile and grant you a Verified badge.
We currently support HVAC, Plumbing, Roofing, and Electrical. More niches will be added soon.
